## Description

This is small fishing town on the East coast of [[Mirakas]]. It has a population of around 500 people. Most of the people here are either fishermen and fisherwomen, or aim to provide services to them, such as tavern-keeps to entertain, blacksmiths and weavers to provide tools, or priests to ensure good omens while sailing.

Of course no good town is complete without some crime. Sleenport had always had some roguish folk who mostly used it as a place of transit, catching rides with local seafarers willing to accept coin. However as of recent it has become somewhat overrun by these folks, who now use it as a base of operations for committing all sorts of crime.
