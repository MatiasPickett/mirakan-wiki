I thought it would be fun to add some achievements to the game.
Each achievement obtained comes with the reward of an Heroic Inspiration, and sometimes other extras.
### Story Achievements
- **Baptized** — Create a name for the party
- **Fangirl** — Speak with each member of [[The Big Four]]
- **Becoming Eternal**  — Do something worthy of a history book
### Combat Achievements
- **Must have been the wind** —  Kill an enemy using the environment
- **Sniper** — Kill an enemy more than 600 ft. away
- **Overkill** — Deal 200 damage in a single turn
- ~~**The First Half** — Complete a Dungeon~~
- **The Other Half** — Slay a Dragon
### Miscellaneous Achievements
- **I meant to do that** — Roll two Nat 20s with disadvantage
- **Oops** — Roll two Nat 1s with advantage
- **Overkill** — Roll a 30 or higher
