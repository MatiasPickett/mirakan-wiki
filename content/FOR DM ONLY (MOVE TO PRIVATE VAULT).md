NPC names **Julius**, he is the currently the second choice to lead the revolution against the elven Empire, after Daalia Kath. Even though he is common born and unlike her has no claim by blood, he from a young age showed a strong patriotism. He was in the Kathian Navy, fought in the **Blue War**, and later became an underground symbol against the oppression of the elves. His current whereabouts are unknown. 

Because of his presence as an anti-elven symbol, it is not uncommon to see vandalism bearing his image and name. Generally represented by a Blue Heart. 

One reason some people dislike Julius, is that he can sometimes be brutal and merciless towards his enemies. Julius is a person who is ruthless and impulsive, but can also be charismatic and sensible. The Men and Women under his command have a huge respect toward him and are very loyal. They wouldn't hesitate to do all sorts of things such as murder, steal, torture and blackmail, all with the justification that what they are doing is for the good of the Kingdom.

Julius became infamous for figuring out and applying several "interrogation" techniques specifically targeted at elves and their militants during the war.
