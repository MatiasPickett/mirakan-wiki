Consuming or administering this Crystal takes an Action.

When you consume this Crystal, if you don't have the ==Fey Ancestry== trait, make a CON save(DC 12), on a failed save you are poisoned for 1 Hour. Whether you succeed on the save or not, you also have blurry vision and a euphoric feeling that gives +1 to all your rolls for 1 Hour.

Also as soon as you consume the crystal, roll on the [[Wild Magic Surge]] table.
