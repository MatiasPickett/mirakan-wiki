So with my experience running the Curse of Strahd Campaign, i think leveling up went up too fast, so i'm gonna do things a bit different.

Officially we are using the **milestone** method of leveling up, which means we don't use XP, instead the party levels up when reaching specific milestones in the story or whenever I feel like the party has earned it.

However I don't want you all waiting all the time for level ups, so I want everyone to understand that ==progress will come in many other ways besides leveling==, this means:
- Knowledge
	- Sometimes knowing something can be a huge advantage for the party, such as a specific enemies weakness or some kind of secret you can blackmail with
- Equipment
	- Weapons 
	- Armor
	- Tools
- Magic Items
	- Potions
	- Scrolls
	- SpellBooks
	- FR Magic Items
- Allies
	- Actual NPCs that join the party
	- Political alliances and favors

In general I'm going to aim for slow leveling, also so that people can learn each of their class features with due time.
