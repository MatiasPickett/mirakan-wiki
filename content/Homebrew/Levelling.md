So with my experience running the previous Campaign, I think leveling up went up too fast, so i'm gonna do things a bit different.

Officially we are using the **milestone** method of leveling up, which means we won't use XP, instead, the party levels up when reaching specific milestones in the story or whenever I feel like the party has earned it.

However I don't want you all waiting all the time for level ups, so I want everyone to understand that ==progress will come in many other ways besides leveling==, this means:
- Knowledge
	- Sometimes knowing can be a huge advantage for the party, such as a specific enemies weakness or some kind of secret you can blackmail someone with
- Equipment
	- Weapons 
	- Armor
	- Tools
	- Money
- Magic Items
	- Potions
	- Scrolls
	- Spell Books
	- Other Magic Items
- Allies
	- Actual NPCs that join the party
	- Political alliances and favors

In general I'm going to aim for **slow leveling**, also so that people can learn each of their class features with due time.
