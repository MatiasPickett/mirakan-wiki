## The World

This campaign is set on the continent of **Mirakas**, a varied and populated continent that has a bumpy history over the last decades.

There once were many kingdoms present on the continent: the **Kingdom of Kath**(Human), the **Garakh Alliance**(Orc), the **Gnome Commonwealth**, etc. But everything changed when 33 years ago, on the beaches of the north-eastern coast arrived fleets of big ships containing creatures that no one had seen before, *Elves*.
These Elves were part of a great Empire from across the ocean and in the following years a War ensued. This war named **The Blue War** led to the fall of the Human **Kingdom of Kath**, and the founding of a new Capitol to this colony of the Elven Empire in **New Olencia**.

Along with these Elves came a plethora of new species that also started to call this new continent home, refer to the "[[Mirakas]]" page for more information.

## Beginning
We start the campaign in the summer of the Year 33 of the new Mirakan Calendar.
The Player Characters will start in the small fishing town of [[Warmshore]].
