## **The Protector**
![[Corellon.webp]]

Corellion is the ancient god of the Elven peoples, and even though they mostly ==aren't worshiped in the Empire anymore==, there are select groups of elves who since ancient times haven't let go of their worship. These groups are sometimes seen as antiquated or cultish by the main stream Elven Population.

The worship of Corellion has only increased in the past few years when several missionaries came to the new world of [[Mirakas]] with the intention of spreading the worship of Corellion.

Corellion embodies all things elven, simultaneously being: warrior and poet, a mage and a bard, and a male and a female.

Corellion is represented wearing a sky-blue cloak, a large amulet displaying a crescent moon within a circle, and a pair of dazzling gauntlets.

### Followers
Clerics and Worshippers value Corellion's values in other people and themselves, these being:
- Strength of Body and Mind
- Self expression through art
- Strong willpower & Free will
