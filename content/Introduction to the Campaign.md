## The World

This campaign is set on the continent of **Mirakas**, a varied and populated continent who’s had a bumpy history over the last decades.

There once was many kingdoms present such as: the **Kingdom of Kath**(Human), the **Garakh Alliance**(Orc), as well as many others, however 33 years ago, on the beaches of the north-eastern coast suddenly arrived fleets of big ships, containing creatures that no one had seen before, Elves.

These Elves were part of a great Empire from across the ocean and in the following years a War ensued. This war led to the fall of the Human **Kingdom of Kath**, and the founding of a new Capitol to this colony of the Elven Empire in **New Olencia**.

Along with these Elves came a plethora of new species that also started to call this new continent home, refer to the "[[Mirakas]]" page for more information.
